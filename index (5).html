Company Overview

Deloitte is one of the world’s leading professional‑services firms, providing audit, consulting, tax, and advisory solutions to clients in more than 150 countries. Our New York headquarters at 30 Rockefeller Plaza serves as a strategic hub for North‑American brand, marketing, and thought‑leadership initiatives.

Position Summary

The Marketing Specialist supports end‑to‑end planning, execution, and optimization of integrated campaigns that elevate Deloitte’s brand and engage key audiences. This role blends creative storytelling with data‑driven insights and offers hands‑on experience across digital, content, and internal‑communication programs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Partner with strategists, designers, and subject‑matter leaders to deliver brand‑aligned campaigns across web, social, email, and events.
  • Translate research and performance data into actionable insights that shape campaign messaging, audience targeting, and KPI tracking.
  • Maintain campaign calendars, manage timelines, and steward deliverables through Deloitte’s review and compliance processes.
  • Develop and curate content—articles, social posts, infographics, email copy—ensuring consistency with Deloitte’s tone and visual identity.
  • Support vendor and agency coordination, budget tracking, and dashboard reporting for active initiatives.
  • Monitor industry trends and competitor activity to identify opportunities for differentiation and innovation.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, Media, or a related discipline.
  • Professional experience contributing to digital, brand, or content‑marketing initiatives in a fast‑paced environment.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, with a keen eye for detail and brand consistency.
  • Familiarity with CMS, analytics, and social‑publishing platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Adobe, Salesforce, Sprinklr, or similar).
  • Ability to balance creativity with data‑driven decision‑making and to collaborate effectively across multifunctional teams.

Preferred Experience

  • Exposure to B2B or professional‑services marketing environments.
  • Experience building dashboards and tracking campaign performance metrics.
  • Interest in internal‑communications and brand‑culture initiatives.
